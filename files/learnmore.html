LOCATION
San Francisco High School (SFHS) is a public educational institution located in Bago Bantay, Quezon City.
© Viel Tiffany Frederizo
HISTORY
San Francisco High School began its journey on June 9, 1954, as a humble annex of Quezon City High School with only 90 students and 6 faculty members. Despite initial limitations with classes held in the rundown Pizano building, the school persevered. By September 30, 1958, it gained independence and held its first graduation ceremony. Enrollment flourished, reaching 5,334 students by 1970, necessitating an expansion from the initial 3 sections and 6 teachers to 144 sections and 263 teachers in 1971. The school's name underwent a brief change to Don Mariano Marcos High School in November 1975, but it was rightfully restored to San Francisco High School in October 1986, a name it proudly carries to this day.
